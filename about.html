Our Story

Our Story awnsr was founded in 2025 by a team of researchers and engineers who saw a recurring problem across industries — businesses are sitting on valuable data but have no simple way to ask questions of it and get trusted answers back. We built DSSAS, the Domain-Specific Smart Answer System, to solve exactly that.
