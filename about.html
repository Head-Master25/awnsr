About awnsr

Making business intelligence accessible to every organisation

Our Story

Our Story awnsr was founded in 2025 by a team of researchers and engineers bringing over 20 years of combined experience in data analytics and data interoperability. Together, we saw a recurring problem across industries. Businesses are sitting on valuable data but have no simple way to ask questions of it and get trusted answers back. We built DSSAS, the Domain-Specific Smart Answer System, to solve exactly that.

Our Core Values

Transparency

Every answer DSSAS delivers is traceable back to its source data. We believe AI should show its working, not just produce outputs, but provide the evidence behind them so your team can act with confidence..

Trust

Our outputs are verified, auditable, and grounded in your actual data. We engineer against hallucinations by design, ensuring decision-makers receive reliable intelligence they can stake business outcomes on.

Accessibility

We eliminate the technical barrier between your teams and your data. Through a conversational natural language interface, anyone in your organisation can query complex datasets including numerical figures, financial data, and operational metrics and receive clear, actionable insights without needing SQL knowledge, BI tools, or data science expertise. Your data speaks your language.

Domain Depth

Generic AI gives generic answers. We specialise in agriculture, healthcare, and telecommunications - building systems that understand the terminology, workflows, and data structures unique to your industry.

Our Mission

Our Mission To make business intelligence accessible - giving SMBs the same data querying power that large enterprises pay millions for, through a natural language interface that is verified, transparent, and trusted.
